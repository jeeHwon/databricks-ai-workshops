# Academic Integrity Policy

EduPath Academy is committed to fostering an environment of honesty and integrity. All students must adhere to the highest standards of academic conduct. Plagiarism, cheating, and unauthorized collaboration are strictly prohibited.

## Violations

- Plagiarism: Submitting work that is not your own.
- Cheating: Using unauthorized materials during exams.
- Collusion: Collaborating without permission.

## Consequences

Violations may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or expulsion.
